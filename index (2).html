About us

Magnum has been with you since 2007. Over 15 years, we have opened 200+ branches in fourteen cities of Kazakhstan. Over the past years, we have become the largest trade and retail chain in Kazakhstan and continue to develop dynamically, striving to provide our beloved customers with a large assortment of quality goods, low prices and the best service. We want everyone in our country to enjoy the best shopping experience. We grow and develop together with our customers. Together with our customers, we create opportunities to develop domestic production and improve the quality of domestic goods.
